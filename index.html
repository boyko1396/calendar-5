Scandalous charity campaign. The "Uncensored" calendar from Channel 5

For the Day of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, Channel 5 released a special calendar, featuring Channel 5 employees.

The project aims to collect at least a million hryvnias for walkie-talkies for the soldiers of the Armed Forces, made a real noise in Ukraine.

Want the physical "Uncensored" calendar from Channel 5? Take three steps

  1. Donate 57 or more Euro / USD
    • via credit card, Google Pay, Apple Pay
    • via Paypal: paypal@5.ua. Please add the note "On the walkie-talkie"
    • via SEPA
  2. Fill out your address and contact details in the order form and attach a sectionshot of your donation receipt.
  3. Pick up the calendar from your local post office.

All funds will be spent to buy portable two way radios for Armed Forces of Ukraine

The DP4400 portable two-way radio delivers unrivaled voice and data communications with optional integrated GPS and Bluetooth. This feature-rich radio delivers plus best-in-class audio that includes Intelligent Audio and customizable voice announcements for status notifications.

Available in UHF and VHF frequency bands, the DP4400 / DP4401 is easy to use and features up to 32 channel capacity, five programmable buttons, dedicated emergency button, IP57 specifications for submersibility and FM or TIA intrinsically safe options.
