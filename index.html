A Cheeky Charity campaign: Channel 5’s “Uncensored” Calendar

In honour of ‘The Day of Ukrainian Armed Forces’, Channel 5 released a scandalously unflinching calendar, consensually featuring its own employees.

The project has already collected 4.5 million hryvnias. All funds are spent to purchase two-way-radios for Ukrainian Armed Forces. 130 items has already been sent. Campaign is running.

Want to get the "Uncensored" gift from Channel 5?

  1. Donate 80 or more Euro / USD
    • via credit card, Google Pay, Apple Pay
    • via Paypal: paypal@5.ua. Please add the note "On the walkie-talkie"
    • via SEPA
  2. Select the gift(s) you would like to get as our gratitude in return.
  3. Fill out your address, contact details in the order form. Attach the screenshot of your donation confirmation screen. List the gift(s) in the comment field.
  4. Pick up the parcel at your local post office.

If you are lacking payment methods or experiencing some issues with the form above please email us at lyst@5.ua
